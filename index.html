MPG Design Roadmap

Career Progression Framework

Click any level to explore detailed expectations, skills, and growth signals for each stage of your design career at MPG.

Level 1

Junior Graphic Designer

Supports projects through production and basic design tasks

+

Scope of Work

Supports projects through production and basic design tasks; learns process and tools.

Creative & Technical Skills

Foundational design principles; developing Adobe CC, Figma, and After Effects skills.

Collaboration & Leadership

Works closely with Seniors; receptive to feedback; eager to learn.

Strategy & Impact

Understands basic MPG brand goals and audiences.

Growth Signals

  • Completes tasks accurately and on time
  • Curious about tools and workflow
  • Building foundational Adobe CC + Figma/AE skills
  • Applies feedback quickly
  • Steady growth in technical competency
Level 2

Graphic Designer

Executes assigned projects independently with guidance

+

Scope of Work

Executes assigned projects independently with guidance; manages workload effectively.

Creative & Technical Skills

Strong Adobe CC fluency; developing Figma/XD and After Effects capability.

Collaboration & Leadership

Collaborates well cross-functionally; dependable teammate.

Strategy & Impact

Applies creative solutions that support marketing performance.

Growth Signals

  • Produces high-quality creative independently
  • Strong Photoshop/Illustrator/InDesign capabilities
  • Growing motion/digital/interactive skills
  • Proposes creative alternatives
  • Handles multiple deliverables efficiently
Level 3

Senior Graphic Designer

Leads projects end-to-end and mentors designers

+

Scope of Work

Leads projects end-to-end; contributes to concepts; mentors designers informally.

Creative & Technical Skills

Deep expertise in at least one discipline (print, motion, coding, or digital).

Collaboration & Leadership

Provides critique, raises team standards, supports cross-functional partners.

Strategy & Impact

Improves workflows, problem-solving, and creative quality.

Growth Signals

  • Leads full campaigns independently
  • Advanced Adobe + Figma + AE + optional HTML/CSS
  • Reliable creative judgment
  • Coaches and shares best practices
  • Elevates consistency and quality of output
Level 4

Principal Designer

Sets the creative bar across domains and drives innovation

+

Scope of Work

Sets the creative bar across multiple domains; drives innovation; partners with leadership.

Creative & Technical Skills

Cross-disciplinary mastery; expert in systems, art direction, and strategy.

Collaboration & Leadership

Mentors Seniors Designers and Graphic Designers.

Strategy & Impact

Influences MPG-wide brand direction and creative evolution.

Growth Signals

  • Creative visionary across mediums
  • Raises creative standards org-wide
  • Guides long-term creative evolution
  • Collaborates at the highest strategic level
  • Recognized as a creative authority at MPG
Level 5

Design Director

Leads MPG Design's people, operations, and creative excellence

+

Scope of Work

Leads MPG Design's people, operations, and creative excellence.

Creative & Technical Skills

Broad design knowledge; expertise in systems, process, and cross-functional leadership.

Collaboration & Leadership

Develops talent; guides Leads and Seniors; represents Design across the business.

Strategy & Impact

Defines overall creative strategy and organizational vision.

Growth Signals

  • Builds a strong creative culture
  • Develops future leaders
  • Aligns Design with cross-functional departments
  • Improves workflows and operational efficiency
  • Shapes long-term creative and brand strategy
Level 6

Creative Director

Oversees company-wide creative vision across brands and channels

+

Scope of Work

Oversees company-wide creative vision across brands and channels.

Creative & Technical Skills

Visionary thinker; expert across branding, digital, storytelling, and conceptual development.

Collaboration & Leadership

Leads Design, Studio, and Marketing Creative alignment.

Strategy & Impact

Defines MPG's creative identity and long-term brand direction.

Growth Signals

  • Sets company-wide creative vision
  • Mentors senior leadership
  • Maintains excellence across all channels
  • Aligns brand strategy across departments
  • Drives company-wide creative innovation
