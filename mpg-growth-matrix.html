MPG Design Growth Matrix

Building exceptional designers through clarity, craft, and career growth

Career Progression Framework

Click any level to explore detailed expectations, skills, and growth signals for each stage of your design career at MPG.

Level 1

Junior Graphic Designer

Supports projects through production and basic design tasks

+

Scope of Work

Supports projects through production and basic design tasks; learns process and tools.

Creative & Technical Skills

Foundational understanding of design principles; developing Adobe CC, Figma, After Effects fluency.

Collaboration & Leadership

Works closely with Seniors; open to feedback; eager to learn.

Strategy & Impact

Understands MPG's creative purpose and audience basics.

Growth Signals

  • Completes assigned tasks accurately and on time, with attention to detail.
  • Demonstrates curiosity about MPG's creative tools and systems.
  • Building foundational skills in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign; beginning exposure to Figma and After Effects.
  • Seeks feedback actively and applies it quickly.
  • Shows steady growth in technical fluency and creative confidence.
Level 2

Graphic Designer

Executes assigned projects independently with guidance

+

Scope of Work

Executes assigned projects independently with guidance; manages workload effectively.

Creative & Technical Skills

Solid foundation in design, motion, and digital principles; handles full promo campaign assets; Advanced knowledge of PSD, AI, INDD; Developing knowledge of Figma/XD, AE.

Collaboration & Leadership

Collaborates cross-functionally; receptive to feedback; reliable team player.

Strategy & Impact

Applies creative solutions to drive marketing performance.

Growth Signals

  • Produces high-quality creative independently with minimal oversight.
  • Demonstrates advanced use of Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign; growing fluency in Figma/XD and After Effects.
  • Balances multiple campaign deliverables efficiently while meeting deadlines.
  • Begins proposing creative alternatives and design optimizations.
  • Collaborates effectively with copywriters, marketers, and developers.
Level 3

Senior Graphic Designer

Owns end-to-end design projects and mentors peers

+

Scope of Work

Owns end-to-end design projects; contributes to creative concepts; mentors peers informally.

Creative & Technical Skills

Advanced skill in at least one discipline (print, motion, code, or digital). Advanced knowledge of Adobe CS, XD/Figma, AE, HTML, CSS.

Collaboration & Leadership

Mentors designers; collaborates with marketing and CD.

Strategy & Impact

Contributes to creative problem-solving and process improvement.

Growth Signals

  • Leads projects end-to-end with minimal direction.
  • Demonstrates advanced cross-tool expertise across Adobe Creative Suite, Figma/XD, After Effects, and HTML/CSS.
  • Coaches other designers on best practices, workflow efficiency, and creative standards.
  • Pushes creative boundaries while maintaining brand alignment.
  • Recognized as a trusted partner who elevates team craft and output quality.
Level 4

Principal Designer

Creative authority driving innovation and quality

+

Scope of Work

Creative authority in multiple domains; leads visual quality and innovation.

Creative & Technical Skills

Expert-level craft across mediums and all design software; drives experimentation and innovation.

Collaboration & Leadership

Coaches seniors and designers; partners with Head of Design and CD.

Strategy & Impact

Shapes MPG's visual standards and elevates creative direction.

Growth Signals

  • Expert-level proficiency across design, motion, and interactive tools; leverages them to experiment and innovate.
  • Mentors senior and mid-level designers, improving team craft and confidence.
  • Defines creative benchmarks for MPG Design output and quality.
  • Improves tools, processes, and team workflows for higher efficiency.
  • Shapes MPG's visual standards and inspires creative excellence.
Level 5

Head of Design

Leads MPG Design team operations and creative excellence

+

Scope of Work

Leads MPG Design team operations and creative excellence.

Creative & Technical Skills

Broad design knowledge; understands systems, process, and team operations.

Collaboration & Leadership

Leads culture and mentorship; aligns creative with marketing and brand strategy.

Strategy & Impact

Defines design strategy and direction for MPG Design.

Growth Signals

  • Develops and articulates a clear design vision aligned with business goals.
  • Grows and mentors a high-performing team of designers and creative specialists.
  • Improves operational systems, efficiency, and creative quality across the org.
  • Represents MPG Design in cross-departmental discussions and planning.
  • Creates an inclusive, inspiring, and high-standard creative culture.
Level 6

Creative Director

Oversees holistic creative vision across MPG brands

+

Scope of Work

Oversees holistic creative vision across MPG brands; connects brand, marketing, and storytelling.

Creative & Technical Skills

Creative visionary with expert cross-media understanding.

Collaboration & Leadership

Provides leadership across Design, Studio, and MarTech.

Strategy & Impact

Shapes company-wide creative strategy and visual identity.

Growth Signals

  • Defines and communicates MPG's creative vision across brands and platforms.
  • Mentors and empowers design and creative leaders.
  • Sets and maintains creative excellence across all visual and brand touchpoints.
  • Shapes creative strategy in collaboration with senior marketing and business leadership.
  • Champions design as a strategic driver of growth, storytelling, and brand value.
